What is it?

I've been interested in pinhole camera, solargraphs and camera tomfoolary for a long time. This year I've made 10 pinhole camera from old airrifle pellet tins, tinfoil and insulation tape. I intent to install them around the site, swapping the paper in them periodically (maybe daily) and posting the results here.

I've no idea if it'll work, or what the images will be like, but isn't that hal thr fun?
